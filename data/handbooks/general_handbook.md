### Ordering Process

Placing an order on Shoplytic is simple and secure:

1. **Browse or search for products** - Use our AI-powered search to find what you're looking for
2. **View detailed product information** - Check specifications, images, and reviews
3. **Add to cart** - Select items and add them to your shopping cart
4. **Review your cart** - Verify items, quantities, and total price
5. **Provide shipping information** - You'll need to provide:
   - Full name
   - Complete address
   - City
   - Zip code
6. **Complete payment** - Use a valid voucher code to complete your purchase
7. **Receive order confirmation** - You'll get an order confirmation with your order ID

**Important Notes:**
- Shipping information is required before payment can be processed
- If you've provided shipping information before, it will be saved and reused for future orders
- Payment is completed using voucher codes only
- Once an order is placed, you'll receive a confirmation with your order details

## Returns & Refunds

### Return Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy, we're here to help.

**Return Window**: 30 days from the date of delivery

**Eligible Items**: Most items in new, unused condition with original packaging and tags

**Non-Returnable Items**:
- Personalized or customized products
- Items damaged by misuse
- Products without original packaging
- Digital products (once downloaded)

### Return Process

1. Log into your account and navigate to "My Orders"
2. Select the item you wish to return
3. Click "Return Item" and follow the prompts
4. Print the prepaid return label
5. Package the item securely with original packaging
6. Drop off at the designated carrier location
7. Once received and inspected, we'll process your refund

### Refund Processing

- Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive your return
- Original shipping costs are non-refundable (unless the item was defective or incorrect)
- Refunds are issued to the original payment method
- You'll receive an email confirmation when your refund is processed

### Exchanges

We currently process exchanges through returns and new purchases. Return the item you don't want and place a new order for the item you prefer.
